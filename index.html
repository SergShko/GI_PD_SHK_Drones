      Methodology & sources

      All 77 cases are corroborated by geolocation, police statements, or secondary reporting from credible outlets: AP, Reuters, InSight Crime, CSIS, War on the Rocks, Militant Wire, G1, O Globo, El Tiempo, Semana, Folha de São Paulo, Prensa Libre, El Comercio, and others.

      Platforms searched include Twitter/X, Telegram, Facebook, YouTube, and OSINT aggregators. Duplicate entries across sources were merged; only the most detailed, corroborated version is retained.

      Groups with no verified or credible drone activity in the reviewed research: MS-13, Barrio 18, Gulf Clan / Clan del Golfo, Tren de Aragua, Los Choneros, Los Lobos, and other Central American maras.
